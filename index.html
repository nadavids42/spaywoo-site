Why SpayWoo?

Small local efforts can change thousands of lives.

Stray cats are a community issue, but they are also individual lives. SpayWoo exists to make helping them feel less overwhelming by organizing people around clear, compassionate next steps.

We are starting simple: volunteer coordination, education, intake, and support for spay/neuter efforts. As the organization grows, this site will become a home for forms, resources, events, and impact stories.
