Worcester-area community cat support

Tough lives deserve a fighting chance.

SpayWoo is a grassroots effort helping stray and community cats through spay/neuter coordination, local volunteers, and practical support for people who want to help but do not know where to start.

Volunteer with us Learn the mission

The mission

Lowering overpopulation. Building compassion.

Stray cats are not invisible. They are neighbors living hard lives outdoors. SpayWoo is being built to make local help easier to organize: fewer future litters, more coordinated care, and better outcomes for cats and communities.

Built for the cats people see behind restaurants, under porches, near dumpsters, and around the blocks they walk every day.

What we are building

Simple, local, useful.

01

Spay/neuter coordination

Help connect community cats with the support needed to reduce future litters and stabilize local colonies.

02

Volunteer network

Organize neighbors who can help with transport, trapping support, outreach, admin work, supplies, and local coordination.

03

Community resources

Create clear guidance for residents who find stray cats and want to take responsible, compassionate action.
